Sultan Fateh Ali Sahab Tipu

Tipu Sultan, also known as the Tiger of Mysore, was born on November 20, 1750, in Devanahalli, near present-day Bangalore, India. He was the eldest son of Sultan Hyder Ali of Mysore, a powerful ruler in southern India. Tipu Sultan succeeded his father as the ruler of the Kingdom of Mysore in 1782 after his death.
He is also a controversial figure, with opinions divided on his legacy, seen by some as a freedom fighter and by others as a ruthless dictator.
